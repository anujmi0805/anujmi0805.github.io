FAQs

What is net worth?
Net worth is the difference between your total assets and total liabilities, representing your financial position.
How do I calculate net worth?
Subtract your total liabilities from your total assets using the provided calculator.
Why is net worth important?
It provides a snapshot of financial health and helps in planning, saving, and investing effectively.
How often should I check my net worth?
It is recommended to check it quarterly or at least twice a year to track progress and adjust plans.
Can net worth be negative?
Yes, if your liabilities exceed your assets, your net worth will be negative, indicating a need to reduce debt.
Does net worth include property?
Yes, all owned assets including property, investments, cash, and valuables should be included.
Can net worth increase without earning more?
Yes, by reducing liabilities or increasing the value of existing assets like investments or property.
Is net worth useful for planning retirement?
Absolutely, understanding net worth helps in retirement planning, debt management, and achieving financial goals.
