Investment Knowledge

Simple financial rules and concepts that help you understand growth, time, and decision-making in investing.

Rule of 72 Rule of 114 Rule of 144 Cost of Delay

Why These Simple Rules Matter

In investing, not every decision needs complex calculations. Some of the most powerful insights come from simple rules that quickly help you estimate outcomes and understand long-term effects.

These rules act as mental shortcuts. They help you make faster decisions without losing clarity, especially when comparing investment options or understanding time-based growth.

Understanding Time and Growth

Most financial success depends on two things: time and consistency. These knowledge tools are designed to highlight how money grows over time and how delays or wrong decisions can impact your financial journey.

Whether it's estimating how fast your money doubles or understanding the cost of waiting, these concepts provide clarity without overwhelming complexity.

Where to Use These Concepts

You can use these rules while planning investments, comparing returns, or simply building financial awareness. They are especially useful when you need quick approximations without detailed calculations.

These concepts are not replacements for calculators but serve as guiding principles that support better financial thinking.
