Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how WeDA collects, uses, and protects your information while you interact with our platform.

Introduction

WeDA is a multi-sector digital platform designed to provide structured tools, calculators, and informational systems across domains such as finance, education, health, utilities, and more.

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how data is handled when you use our services, ensuring transparency, trust, and compliance with applicable standards.

Information We Collect

WeDA does not require users to create accounts or provide personal information for general usage.

However, we may collect limited non-personal data such as:

  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • General geographic region (non-precise)

This data helps us improve platform performance and user experience.

Cookies

WeDA uses cookies to enhance user experience and analyze traffic. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us understand user behavior and preferences.

Third-party services, including Google AdSense, may also use cookies to serve relevant advertisements based on user activity.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Google AdSense & Third-Party Services

We may display advertisements through Google AdSense and other advertising partners. These services may use cookies and tracking technologies to provide relevant ads.

Google may use the DoubleClick cookie to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites.

Users can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

How We Use Information

Collected data is used strictly for:

  • Improving platform performance and usability
  • Analyzing user interaction patterns
  • Enhancing content relevance and structure

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal data to third parties.

Data Security

WeDA implements reasonable technical measures to protect data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

External Links

Our platform may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party sites.

Users are advised to review the privacy policies of any external websites they visit.

Your Consent

By using WeDA, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or platform improvements.

Users are encouraged to review this page regularly.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at:

Email: support.weda@gmail.com
